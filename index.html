Custard Caramel Pudding

A delectable, creamy custard dessert with a layer of clear caramel sauce.

Ingredients:

Caramel-

  • 160g(5.6 ounces) Sugar
  • 2 tablespoons Water

Custard-

  • 6 Egg yolks
  • 6 Whole eggs
  • 1000mL Milk
  • 180g Sugar
  • 400g Heavy cream
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract

Procedure:

  • Step 1- Add 160g of sugar along with 2 tbsp of water into a small saucepan
  • Step 2- Stir and set on medium heat
  • Step 3- Stir occasionally until the mixture turns golden-brown, then turn off the heat
  • Step 4- Prepare 10 ramekins and pour the caramel equally into all of them
  • Step 5- Into a large bowl, gently whisk together six egg yolks and six whole eggs till combined
  • Step 6- Set the bowl aside, and into a pot, add 1000mL of milk with 180g of sugar and stir
  • Step 7- Into the mixture, add 400g of heavy cream
  • Step 8- Set the pot on medium heat, and stir the contents constantly to prevent them for burning or sticking to the pot
  • Step 9- Heat the mixture until the milk is just warm(not too hot)
  • Step 10- Gradually add the perpared mixture to the egg mixture. Make sure to not whisk it too hard to ensure a smooth mixture
  • Step 11- Add 2tsp of vanilla extract and whisk
  • Step 12- Pass the mixture through a sieve to get rid of bubbles
  • Step 13- Pour the mixture into all the ramekins and place them in a baking dish in a hot water bath(fill the baking dish with hot water halfway)
  • Step 14- Bake at 150°C(300°F) for 45 to 50 minutes
  • Step 15- Refrigerate for three hours
  • Step 16- To get the pudding out of the ramekins, lightly score a knife around the edges and flip it over onto a plate

Enjoy~
